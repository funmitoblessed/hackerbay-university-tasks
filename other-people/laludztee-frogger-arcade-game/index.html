Welcome to my Classic Arcade Game!

Guide

  • The bugs are your enemies. Avoid them!
  • There are 10 levels in this game. You win when you are able to cross to the blue tiles (water) in each level
  • Collectibles boost your score, make sure to pick them up. The gem and key add 5 points and 15 points respectively to your score
  • Use arrow keys to move player around on laptops or swipe up, down, left or right on touch screen devices
